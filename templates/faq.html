Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about VolunteerHub

What is VolunteerHub?

VolunteerHub is a platform designed for college students to discover, participate in, and earn rewards for volunteering activities. We connect students with meaningful opportunities while gamifying the volunteering experience through a points-based reward system.

How does VolunteerHub work?

It's simple! Sign up as a student, browse available volunteer events, register for ones that interest you, and attend them. After completion, you'll earn points based on the event's duration and impact. These points can later be redeemed for exciting rewards!

Who can use VolunteerHub?

VolunteerHub is primarily designed for college students looking to make a positive impact in their communities. Event organizers and college administrators can also use the platform to create and manage volunteer opportunities.

How do I register for an event?

After logging in, browse the Events page, select an event that interests you, and click the "Register" button. You'll receive a confirmation and any additional information needed for the event.

Can I cancel my registration?

Yes, you can cancel your registration up to 24 hours before the event starts. Go to "My Registrations" in your dashboard and click the "Cancel" button next to the event you wish to withdraw from.

Are there limits to how many events I can join?

There's no limit to how many events you can participate in! However, some events may have capacity restrictions, so we encourage registering early to secure your spot.

How are points calculated?

Points are primarily based on the hours volunteered and the impact level of the event. For example, a 2-hour event might earn you 20 points, while a full-day event could award 50-100 points. Special events or leadership roles may earn bonus points.

What rewards can I redeem with my points?

Rewards vary from campus merchandise, dining vouchers, and exclusive event tickets to official volunteering certificates. Check the Rewards section regularly as we update offerings frequently!

Do my points expire?

Points remain valid throughout your academic year. At the start of each new academic year, a small percentage may expire to encourage regular participation. Check your Points dashboard for details on any upcoming expirations.

How do I create an account?

Click on the "Sign Up" button, enter your college email address, create a password, and fill in your basic information. Verify your email address, and you're ready to start volunteering!

Can I update my profile information?

Yes, you can update most of your profile information by accessing your account settings. However, some information like your student ID may require contacting an administrator for changes.

Is my personal information secure?

Absolutely! We prioritize data security and comply with all relevant privacy regulations. Your personal information is encrypted and never shared with third parties without your consent.
